# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 4, 2026**

## Overview

AgentShield is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### Web Scanner
- **Code you paste is NOT stored.** All scanning happens in your browser.
- No code is transmitted to our servers when using the free web scanner.
- We do not collect, store, or log the code you scan.

### Analytics
- We may use privacy-respecting analytics (like Plausible or no analytics at all).
- We don't use cookies for tracking.

### Pro/Enterprise Features
- API usage may be logged for rate limiting purposes only.
- Logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days.
- We never sell your data to third parties.

## Data Security

- All communications are encrypted via HTTPS.
- We follow security best practices.
- The tool itself is open source for transparency.

## Your Rights

- Request deletion of any data we hold about you.
- Access any data associated with your account.
- Opt out of any non-essential communications.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Agent-Shield-AI/agentshield).
